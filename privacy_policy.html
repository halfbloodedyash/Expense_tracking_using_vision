Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 30, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how AI Expense Tracker Bot ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our WhatsApp-based expense tracking service ("Service").

By using our WhatsApp bot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

  • WhatsApp Phone Number: To identify and communicate with you
  • Message Metadata: Timestamp, message ID, and delivery status
  • Usage Analytics: Bot interaction patterns and command usage

2.2 Information You Provide

  • Expense Data: Amount, description, merchant, category, and date
  • Receipt Images: Photos of receipts you send for processing
  • Text Messages: Expense descriptions and commands you send

2.3 AI-Generated Data

  • Extracted Receipt Data: Information processed from receipt images using AI
  • Categorized Expenses: Automatically categorized expense data
  • Spending Insights: AI-generated analysis of your spending patterns

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Services

  • Process and categorize your expenses
  • Extract data from receipt images using AI technology
  • Generate spending insights and analytics
  • Respond to your commands and queries
  • Store expense history for reporting

3.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve bot functionality
  • Enhance AI accuracy for receipt processing
  • Develop new features based on user needs

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

  • Expense data is stored securely in encrypted databases
  • Receipt images are processed temporarily and not permanently stored
  • Data is retained for the duration of your active use of the service

4.2 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption for data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • No sharing of individual user data with third parties

5. Third-Party Services

Our bot integrates with the following third-party services:

5.1 AI Processing Services

  • Google Gemini Vision: For receipt image processing
  • Groq API: For natural language processing

These services process data according to their respective privacy policies.

5.2 WhatsApp Business API

  • Meta (WhatsApp): For message delivery and communication

Subject to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

6. Your Data Rights

6.1 Access and Control

  • Request access to your stored expense data
  • Modify or delete incorrect expense entries
  • Export your expense data in standard formats

6.2 Data Deletion

  • Stop using the bot to cease new data collection
  • Request complete data deletion by contacting us
  • Data will be deleted within 30 days of request

6.3 Communication Preferences

  • You can stop receiving messages by blocking the bot
  • Commands to opt-out of specific notifications

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

7.1 We DO NOT:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Share individual expense data with other users
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Share data with unauthorized parties

7.2 We MAY share data when:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect our rights or safety
  • You explicitly consent to sharing
  • In anonymized, aggregated form for research

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data during international transfers.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of material changes through the WhatsApp bot or other communication methods.

11. Data Retention

  • Active Users: Data retained while you actively use the service
  • Inactive Users: Data deleted after 12 months of inactivity
  • Deleted Accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days

12. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Email: contact2yash@protonmail.com
  • WhatsApp: Message our bot directly

13. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the EU/EEA, our legal basis for processing includes:

  • Contract Performance: To provide expense tracking services
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve and maintain our service
  • Consent: For optional features and communications

Our WhatsApp bot does not use cookies or web tracking technologies. All interactions occur through WhatsApp's secure messaging platform.
